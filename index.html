Instructions

Press the "start" button on the right to start the game. Your Pokemon will begin to get hungry, bored, and tired.

Your Pokemon CANNOT be fed or played with while it is sleeping. Your Pokemon will automatically wake up after it is fully rested, or you can press "sleep" button to awaken it prematurely.

Your Pokemon will evolve into its later forms once it lives past a certain age. Your Pokemon will get hungry, tired, and bored at a higher rate with each evolution.

Good Luck!
